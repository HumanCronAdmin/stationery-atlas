About StationeryAtlas

Fountain pens have Goulet Pens and r/fountainpens. Ballpoint pens have nothing. Japan makes the best ballpoint pens on Earth — Uni, Pilot, Zebra, Pentel, Tombow — but there's no single place to compare them. Until now. Built in Tokyo with first-hand testing at Itoya Ginza and Maruzen.
